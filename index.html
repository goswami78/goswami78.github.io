About me

Thank you for visiting my site. I am an assistant professor in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering (MAE) at The Ohio State University. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of Maryland (UMD), USA, under the supervision of Prof. Derek A. Paley in 2020. Prior to that, I did my undergrad in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Jadavpur University, India in 2015. I spent two years in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering (MAE) at Princeton University as a postdoc. You can find my dissertation here along with the slides.

SOAR Lab

We work on exciting research problems at the interscetion of dynamical systems, machine learning, and unmanned aerial vehicles. Please visit our lab page for more information.
